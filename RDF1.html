Introduction

Roshan Development Foundation (RDF) is a non-profit, non-government organization that works as a catalyst and enabler for promoting human dignity and human security. RDF brings together, among its Board of Directors and team, a combination of dedicated and experienced professionals, academics, journalists, community developers, and human and women rights activists/promoters.

Our mission is rooted in the belief that every individual deserves a life of dignity, free from the constraints of inequality and injustice. By fostering strategic partnerships and engaging directly with local communities, we implement sustainable solutions that empower the youth, support women’s rights, and protect vulnerable populations across the region.
