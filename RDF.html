Aims and Objectives

Roshan Development Foundation (RDF) will work as a catalyst and enabler for promoting human dignity. RDF aims to educate and uplift under privileged communities through research, dialogues, and knowledge transfer.

Vision & Mission

Vision: A democratic and just society based upon mutual respect, inclusiveness, equality and social justice.

Mission: Promote participatory democracy and social justice through community development.

Program & Services

Roshan Development Foundation (RDF) is dedicated to fostering inclusive, just, and sustainable communities through its work in socio-economic development, democratic engagement, and social justice. Our programs are designed to empower vulnerable/under privileged populations by addressing systemic inequalities. We prioritize the active participation of women, youth, and minority groups in shaping economic, political, cultural, and social outcomes. RDF leads initiatives that promote gender equity, social cohesion, and youth empowerment—while advocating for inclusive policies and practices across all sectors of society.

Additional Initiatives

Community Mobilization & Awareness

Engaging local communities to challenge discriminatory norms and build inclusive leadership.

Climate Resilience & Disaster Preparedness

Promoting environmental sustainability and responsive disaster management strategies.

Youth Engagement

Establishing platforms for youth expression, leadership, and advocacy to address their specific challenges.

Media & Academic Partnerships

Facilitating collaborations that amplify diverse voices and support evidence-based dialogue.

Cultural Activities & Celebrations

We organize festivals, exhibitions, and inter-community cultural events to celebrate diversity, promote harmony, and strengthen social bonds.

Dialogue

Promoting social cohesion through forums, policy engagement, and campaigns on inclusion, human dignity, and social protection.
